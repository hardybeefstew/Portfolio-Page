About Me

My name is Marc and I am currently living near Eugene, OR. I am an adventurer. Maybe not in the typical sense of world travels, but an adventurer all the same. I enjoy continually exploring and learning new things, while growing in areas I am already familiar with.

I enjoy reading, writing, playing music, running, and hiking/camping. I also love solving puzzles and enjoy the satisfaction of seeing something I have built with my own hands. While I have had past experience working in healthcare, retail, and with religious organizations, I have embarked on a new journey as a web developer after rediscovering the latter loves of mine.

Though I am closer to a beginner on the spectrum of web development, feel free to contact me if you're looking to hire an aspiring web developer or if you simply have any questions/comments about myself and/or my work.
